Art, Technology & Web3
 

As summer fades in Chicago, I'm thrilled to share some exciting updates from the past quarter. Aside from developing a robot for a startup that pulls 10,000lb, I had the incredible opportunity to participate in the first Ethereum conference in the city.

Whale Watcher (2023), courtesy Amay Kataria

At ETHChicago, I developed Whale Watcher, a groundbreaking data visualization tool for Web3 artists, collectors, and gallerists. Whale Watcher allows artists to discover the major collectors ("Whales") in their NFT collections, providing them with valuable insights about their reach.

I'm proud to share that Whale Watcher scooped the first prize in the Featured Artists and Creatives bounty.
