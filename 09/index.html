Issue #9

Wrapping Up 2023
 

I hope this final transmission of the year reaches you well from the freezing grounds of Chicago.

In this last update for 2023, I want to put some light on an exciting new direction I've embarked on in my practice. Brace yourself for the Metagems project, where I've been exploring a new visual language. I couldn't be more thrilled to give you a sneak peek of what's in store for 2024. And let's take a moment to reminisce about my residency in Norway, which concluded around this time last year.

Meanwhile, I wish you and your family a joyous and festive celebration! May the remainder of the year be filled with warmth and happiness. I look forward to reconnecting with you in 2024 with some exciting new updates.

Warm Regards,
Amay Kataria
