Can we collectively synthesize a space for inclusivity and freedom?
 

I'm pleased to invite you to a solo exhibition of my new project, Supersynthesis. Presented by MuGallery Chicago, it's an interactive audio-visual art installation that invites the audience to create a space for collective participation.

 

In a time plagued by political upheaval and social fragmentation, Supersynthesis aspires an inclusive space, where anybody can leave a trace of their thought with freedom. The exhibition runs from March 11 - April 1, 2022 and below is the schedule for public programming. I hope you can join me in this shared immersive experience.
