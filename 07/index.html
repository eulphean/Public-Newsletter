Are You A Fine Artist?

  "The best ideas come out of the process; they come out of the work itself." - Chuck Close
 

Last year, I had a bit of a realization when I looked at my Artist CV - it didn’t really make much sense. It was a bunch of activities thrown together without any clear code of understanding. So, I decided to design a new way to organize an Artist CV using Notion, a free productivity tool that is integrated into my studio practice. If you’re a creative and haven't heard of Notion yet, don't worry, I won't judge you too harshly.

 

Anyway, this new system allowed me to organize all of my projects, exhibitions, publications, artist talks, and awards in an interconnected way. This has helped me clearly understand how they’ve contributed to my overall creative career.

 

So if you're a fine artist, check out the Minimal Artist CV Notion template. It's an easy-to-use tool that I've created based on everything I learned while designing a new way to manage my Artist CV. I really hope you find it helpful!

 

Until next time.

Take care.

Amay
