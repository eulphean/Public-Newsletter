WORLD THAT AWAITS

  The biota that crawls or lives, within or without the body, is exchanging itself with others, without any national approvals.
 

Dear %recipient_fname%

 

I'm pleased to invite you to my first curatorial project opening this Friday at Mu Gallery (link) in Chicago. World That Awaits is an interdiscplinary group exhibition exploring themes of post-humanism through a range of multimedia works rooted in contemporary and traditional practices. Bringing together the work of six artists Scott Kemp, Sofia Diaz Fernandez, Cody Norman, Jungwoo Lee, Kayla Anderson, and FÁTIMA, the exhibition invites visitors to imagine a world that deviates from its assigned categories, a world that spills beyond meanings and attributes, a world that rejects trajectories ... a world that simply awaits. With a collection of new and existing projects, the show glimpses the horizon of the unknown to explore an intersubjective - interconnected reality, where existence may be in the form of acceptance rather than resistence or resilience.

 

The exhibition will run from August 26 to September 17, 2022.
