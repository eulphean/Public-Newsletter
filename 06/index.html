Issue #6

Browser
 

I hope you're doing well. This year has been off to a great start for me. I'm teaching a class at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago called Media Art Histories and Genealogies. It's inspired me to dive deeper into my medium and share this knowledge with others.

 

On top of that, I just finished the Bridge Program at the Hyde Park Art Center. Now I'm working towards a showcase at the end of the month. Pretty stoked about that!

 

Meanwhile, I want to share some writing and conversations with you that were published this past quarter. Check them out when you have a chance. Thanks for all your support.

 

Wishing you an awesome year ahead!

 

With all my best,

Amay Kataria
